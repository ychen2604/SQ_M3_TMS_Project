Detailed Description

The purpose of this class is to model BuyerTasks. The buyer represents an employee of OSHT who is tasked with requesting Customer contracts from the Contract Marketplace and generating an initial Order or contract. Their chief output is an Order, which is marked for action by the Planner. After the Planner’s work is completed, the Buyer confirms each completed Order and generates an Invoice to the Customer.

  1. Initiates contact with the Contract Marketplace to receive contracts from Customers
  2. Buyer may review existing Customers and accept new Customers (from the Marketplace) into the TMS system
  3. Buyer may initiate a new Order from the Marketplace requests
  4. Buyer may select relevant Cities for the Order. This will nominate Carriers in those Cities for Order completion (which is confirmed by the Planner)
  5. Buyer may review completed Orders and process them for Invoice Generation.
  6. Invoice Generation results in a text file being generated with appropriate billing details. This information is also stored in the TMS database.
